✈️ How It Works

  • Paste dates into space below
  • International: US-INTL for departures, INTL-US for returns
  • Domestic: First route (e.g., ORD-HNL) is departure, reverse (HNL-ORD) is return
  • Example: IAD-DUB: Nov. 11-12 (international departure)
  • Example: DFW-HNL: Nov. 5-7 (domestic departure)
  • Any route without a US departure airport will not be processed
  • Routes highlighted in red have low availability (< 20 dates for any metric), with darker red for critically low counts (< 10)
  • Uncheck the "Include" box to remove the city from downloaded sheet
  • Remember to ALWAYS manually validate the dates
